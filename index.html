Surah Al-Tawbah, Surah Al-Nisa and Silver

It is reported that Umar b. Al-khattab -Allah be pleased with him- wrote (by way of instructions): Learn Surah Al-Bara'ah (At-Tawbah), and teach your women Surah Al-Nur. and have them wear silver for adornment. Sa'id b. Mansur, Al-Sunan #1003; Al-Bayhaqi,Shuaib Al-Iman #2213
